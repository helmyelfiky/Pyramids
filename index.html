Frequently Asked Questions

Where are your manufacturing facilities located?

Our manufacturing facilities are located in the Fayoum and Qalyoub industrial areas, with warehouses in both locations to ensure efficient storage and distribution.

Are your products available for export?

Yes, we export our adhesive products to the Middle East and Africa, with plans to expand into other international markets.

Do you provide after-sales services?

Yes, we offer comprehensive after-sales services to ensure customer satisfaction, including technical support and assistance tailored to your needs.

Do you offer flexible payment plans

Yes, we provide all payment plan options to accommodate the diverse needs of our customers and ensure a smooth purchasing experience.

How can I place an order or inquire about your products?

You can contact us through our website, email, or phone. Our sales team is ready to assist you with your requirements and provide detailed product information.

What regions do you export to?

We currently export to the Middle East and Africa and are actively exploring opportunities to expand into other global markets.
